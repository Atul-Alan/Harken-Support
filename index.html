FAQ

  1. How do I add a birthday?
    Tap Add Birthday, enter the name and date, optionally set a time, and save. You’ll stay on the form so you can add more.
  2. Can I get a reminder before the day?
    Yes. Pick a pre-reminder (e.g., 1 day before). Harken uses local notifications—no internet required.
  3. Why didn’t I get a notification?
    Ensure notifications are allowed for Harken in Settings → Notifications. Keep your device time accurate and Do Not Disturb off.
  4. How do I avoid duplicates?
    If you add the same name and date, Harken nudges you once per session. You can still save if it’s intentional.
  5. How do I back up my data?
    Go to Settings → Export and export as CSV or ICS. Save the file to iCloud Drive/Google Drive.
  6. Does Harken require an account?
    No. All data stays on your device. No sign-in or cloud sync.

Troubleshooting

  1. App stuck on launch (iOS): Force close, then reboot the device. If it persists, uninstall and reinstall Harken. No account is required and all data is local, so export CSV/ICS first if possible.
  2. Notifications not firing: Open the app once after install to grant permissions. Verify alert/badge/sound are enabled. If you set a time, keep the device unlocked at that moment on some OEMs.
  3. Time zone changes: If you traveled, open Harken once to reschedule reminders in the new time zone.
  4. Export issues: On iOS, use the native share sheet and “Save to Files”. On Android, ensure storage permission is granted when prompted.
