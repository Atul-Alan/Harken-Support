Privacy Policy

Harken — Keep the days that matter.

Overview

Harken is an offline-first app. Your data (events, notes, categories) is stored locally on your device. We do not run servers and we do not collect, sell, or share personal data.

Data We Store Locally

  • Event title, date, optional time
  • Optional notes and relationship/category
  • Reminder settings (pre-reminder days, notification on/off)

Local notifications are scheduled on-device using your system time zone.

Data Sharing

When you export CSV or ICS, files are created on your device. If you share them, they leave your device through the method you choose (e.g., email, Files, Drive). We do not receive copies.

Third-Party Services

Harken is built with Flutter and uses local storage (Hive) and system notification APIs. The app does not include analytics or third‑party tracking SDKs.

Data Deletion

Because data is local, uninstalling Harken removes the app and its local database. If you need assistance, contact us at languor_aircrew_9l@icloud.com.
